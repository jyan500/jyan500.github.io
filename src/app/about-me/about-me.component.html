About Me

My name is Jansen Yan and I am a software developer. I recently graduated from University of California, Irvine with a B.S in Computer Science in Summer 2018.

I am currently a software developer at a small team in Southern California and specialize in Full Stack Web Dev. I mainly work in the backend using PHP nowadays, but I am constantly learning and mastering new skills in the frontend and backend. One of my current interests is the Ionic Framework, used to build Hybrid Mobile Applications.


How did you get into Programming?

Growing up in the Bay Area, I lived in the tech-boom era of the early 2000's. I was surrounded by tech, and programming was quickly rising in popularity. However when I was younger, I didn't have much interest in programming, and originally wanted to pursue a career in healthcare. It wasn't until my freshmen year at University of California, Irvine that I found my passion in Computer Science after seeing my friends build cool apps and games in their introductory programming classes. I switched my major from Biological Sciences to Computer Science in my Sophomore Year.

As I continued on through my Computer Science education, I began to learn more theory and exposed myself to different areas of Computer Science such as Operating Systems, Machine Learning and Big Data. I went through tough times during my 4 years, and it definitely wasn't easy. However through it all, what kept me going was that through coding, I was able to take an idea and turn it into reality on my computer screen!

After graduation, I am now starting my career as a software developer and my passion remains the same, to strive to improve, try my best, turn ideas into reality through code!
